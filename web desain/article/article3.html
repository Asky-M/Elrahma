Yogyakarta empty of tourists as COVID-19 takes toll


One hundred percent of would-be visitors have cancelled their trips because of the COVID-19 outbreak in the country, according to the Association of Indonesian Tour and Travel Agencies (ASITA).

“This means we didn't get any visitors since last week until the end of March,” said the head of ASITA's Yogyakarta chapter, Udhi Sudiyanto, in a statement as quoted by tempo.co on Tuesday. more information
